# Podcast Style Guide: Shaan Puri & Sam Parr Style

## Voice & Tone

**Energy Level:** High energy, enthusiastic, engaged
**Formality:** Very casual, conversational, like talking to a friend
**Greeting:** "Yo, what's up!" or similar casual opener

## Language Patterns

### Key Phrases
- "And this is where it gets interesting..."
- "Here's what's crazy..."
- "Here's the beautiful part..."
- "Let's be real about..."
- "The money shot is..."
- "And boom..."
- "Dude..."
- "Think about it..."

### Analogies & Metaphors
- Use business/franchise analogies ("It's like having a franchise model but for...")
- Physical metaphors ("separate safety deposit boxes", "messy garage vs organized workshop")
- Action movie references ("would make a CIA operative proud")

## Content Structure

### Hook & Setup
- Start with a relatable problem or observation
- "So here's what happened..."
- Build curiosity: "And once you see it, you can't unsee it"

### Explaining Concepts
- Break complex ideas into simple patterns
- Use concrete examples from real clients/situations
- Number important points ("Pattern number one:", "First:", "Second:")
- End sections with impact statements ("Here's what's crazy...")

### Transitions
- "And this is where it gets interesting"
- "But here's the thing..."
- "Now here's what's cool..."
- "The crazy part?"

### Conclusions
- Summarize with impact/transformation language
- "This completely changes the game"
- End casually: "Peace out!" or "That's a wrap!"

## Business Focus

### Always Include:
- Real client examples (anonymized if needed)
- Concrete numbers/timeframes when possible
- Business impact/value proposition
- Practical next steps

### Avoid:
- Academic jargon
- Overly technical explanations without context
- Formal business speak
- Lengthy theoretical discussions

## Episode Structure Template

1. **Casual Greeting** (5-10 seconds)
2. **Hook/Problem Setup** (30-45 seconds) 
3. **Core Content** (3-4 minutes)
   - Break into 2-3 main points
   - Use numbered patterns
   - Include concrete examples
   - Add "here's what's crazy" moments
4. **Action/Impact Summary** (30-45 seconds)
5. **Casual Sign-off** (5-10 seconds)

## Example Transformations

**Academic Style:**
"We implemented a systematic approach to client service categorization."

**Shaan/Sam Style:**
"Dude, I figured out this genius way to categorize client services that basically cuts our setup time by 90%."

**Academic Style:**
"The methodology involves three discrete phases."

**Shaan/Sam Style:**
"Here's the beautiful part - it's just three simple steps, and once you see the pattern, you can't unsee it."
